Dashing

Dashing is an omni-directional tool that will propel the player in any given direction relative to the camera

  • Dashing will increase your character's [LINEAR_VELOCITY] attribute significantly
  • Dashing on the ground and in the air has drastically different effects
    • the blue outline indicates that you cannot perform a dash
      •
    • Note that despite being different dashes they share the same cooldown
    • You must be on the ground for the DashCD to reset
  • Dashing does in fact NOT give you invulnerability
  • Neutral Dashing is performed by Dashing without pressing any directional inputs

GROUND DASH

Ground Dash is performed when INITIATING a dash from the ground regardless of where the dash leads you.
  • By default Ground Dash's Cooldown is 0.8s
    • Ground Dashing off a ledge will prevent you from replenishing your Dash CD leaving you helpless in the air
  • Ground Dash is heavily influenced by ground friction. Surfaces like [ICE] has little to no friction
  • Neutral Ground Dash will always give you a forwards dash input

AIR DASH

AIR Dash is performed when INITIATING a dash from the air regardless of where the dash leads you.
  • Air Dashing covers much more distance than Ground Dashing due to the lack of air friction
    • Air Dash increases the ground state's DecelerationTime dramatically for the first 0.2s of activation. This knowledge is crucial to understanding the timing of the [WAVEDASH]
  • Air Dash doesn't have a Cooldown, you cannot dash until you reach a ground state
  • Neutral Air Dash will make you dash where the camera is pointing regardless of XYZ axis
    • This implies that if you dash directly on the Y axis you will not cover much ground horizontally, but much more vertically.
    • Holding forwards before an Air Dash will favor horizontal velocity and add a forwards vector to your dash making it easier to [WAVEDASH] even if the camera is facing the Y axis.

DASH TECH

Dash Techs require a Dash off cooldown to perform

Super Dash

To perform a superdash, ground dash and jump before the dash ends. This will propel you much further than if you had just jumped while running

Superdashes can be extended

Wave Dash

To perform a wave dash, Air Dash diagonally into the ground and jump within 0.2s of the dash's initiation.

To reach the highest speed of wavedashes you have to juggle having the right angle and timing your jump input as early as possible when touching the ground

Wavedashes can be extended

Extended Dashes

Extended Dashes from Ground Dashes are performed by jumping 0.8 seconds after dashing, keeping the superdash momentum and having another dash in the pocket this is indicated by the character being highlighted when the Ground Dash goes off cooldown

All air dashes can be extended by simply dashing into the ground.

Damage Calculation

There are a lot of abilities, Items, status effects and modifiers that can alter your damage. The order in which those changes occur can affect the final damage. When hovering over an ability or an Item in-game a tooltip will appear showing the final output of any changes made to the item.

Strength Calculation Order

Damage Bonuses from ability Modifiers purchased from M.A.I.D and items that directly affect abilities will be applied here

  1. Base damage

    This is the damage written on the ability or item at which it was created

  2. Percent Bonus

    Items in this category are additive to eachother

  3. Flat Bonus Items

    Items or abilities that increase your damage by a flat amount

    Example Items will be added when I come up with more items

  4. [OVERWRITE] Items

    Items of this set will overwrite all previous damage calculations

    Example Items will be added when I come up with more items

  5. Status Effects

    +% bonuses from buffs are added together seperately then multiplied onto the current number

    • Buffs will be listed here...

    • Buffs are applied to abilities/items before they are used. Once an ability is activated, the buff's damage bonus persists for the entire attack duration, even if the buff expires during the attack. However, if you apply a buff while an ability or loot item is already attacking, it will not affect that ongoing attack cycle.

  6. Levels

    +1% bonus for every level gained where level 0 is 0% bonus.

Damage Calculation Order

The calculation for damage onHit. Each of these steps are multiplied on top of the previous step unless specified

  1. Ability or item's strength based on it's adjusted values

    See Strength Calculation Order

  2. Critical damage multiplier

    A critical hit will deal 100% more damage with a base 10% chance of crit onHit

    • Items from the [DECRYPTED] set will prevent you from hitting crits

  3. Debuff Status Effects

    +% bonuses from buffs are added together then multiply the damage after everything else

Cooldown

Cooldown(CD), mutually exclusive to Global Cool Down, is the time required for abilities or items to be available again.

  • The minimum CD for loot that activates on a cooldown is 1s
  • On cooldown resets, there is a 200ms delay between the reset proc and the actual reset
  • [CD REDUCTION ITEM] will not affect loot that cannot be affected by cooldown resets

Global Cool Down

Global cool down (GCD) is the time you have to wait between using GCD abilities. Each ability has a seperate GCD when used

  • The minimum GCD for an ability with a non-zero GCD is 0.3s
  • This system is common in MMORPG's. Other games might use notions like "startup/windup" and "recoveries" that usually prevent two actions overlapping eachother.

Global Cooldown Calculation Order

    2.
  2. Items that multiply GCD
    3.
  3. Items that [OVERRIDE] GCD to a fixed amount
    4.
  4. Buffs that [OVERRIDE] GCD to a flat value
    5.
  5. OnHit Items that [OVERRIDE] GCD to a fixed amount
    6.

Off Global Cool Down

Off Global cool down (oGCD) includes all defensive abilities and 0s GCD abilities. oGCD's can be used at any point in time regardless of non-0s GCD's

  • oGCD attacks usually come from Modifiers that turn the GCD into a normal CoolDown
  • oGCD's fully ignore GCD increases from items

oGCD Length

oGCD's usually have a hidden cooldown of 0.8s for performance reasons
